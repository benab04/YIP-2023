About Us

The Young Innovator's Program serves as a distinguished platform dedicated to fostering the scientific curiosity and problem-solving acumen of aspiring young minds.

This initiative, proudly launched by IIT Kharagpur, aims to recognize and celebrate students who exhibit exceptional creativity and a strong inclination toward scientific research.

  • An exceptional opportunity to present their projects before a distinguished assemblage of esteemed researchers and honored guests.
  • During a two-day visit to IIT Kharagpur, participants will have the distinct privilege of immersing themselves in a rich scientific milieu.
  • We eagerly anticipate your active participation in YIP'23, where your intellectual brilliance is poised to shine.

Eligibility Criteria: This competition exclusively welcomes students currently enrolled in grades VIII through X, with the explicit objective of acknowledging and nurturing their creative potential. We implore you not to allow your exceptional ideas to languish in dormancy.
